Principal Sponsorship Proposal · Nepal AI Summit 2026

Dated 20th March 2026. Addressed to Suman Pokharel, Deputy CEO, Global IME Bank Limited. Proposal issued by The Startup Network Nepal for principal sponsorship of Nepal AI Summit 2026, in collaboration with Oorja World (USA), accelerated by AWS, with US Embassy Nepal as Strategic Partner.

Why This Summit Matters

Nepal is entering a decisive digital transformation phase, where AI will shape banking, fintech, digital payments, risk management, and financial inclusion. The summit convenes national and international AI leaders, regulators, policymakers, innovators, investors, developers, and industry experts.

Strategic Relevance for Global IME Bank

As one of Nepal's leading financial institutions, Global IME Bank can use this platform to strengthen leadership in AI-driven banking transformation, digital innovation partnerships, and smart financial services aligned with Nepal's AI-powered economy.

Event Report 2023-26

Activities Conducted by The Startup Network Nepal

1. India-Nepal Economic Partnership Summit

TSN partnered with PHD Chamber of Commerce for 2023 and 2024 annual summits to deepen economic engagement across remittance, trade, startups, and policy convergence.

2. Startup Catalyst Program (2023)

Incubated Sayapatri Technologies, Clueless Nepal, GharMa Doctor, and Intern Nepal with mentor-led organizational and financial readiness support. Makeup Durbar accelerated to first seed round.

3. Startup Catalyst Program (2024 Sessions)

Advanced cohort sessions with experts including Vikram Singh (Aloft), Mark Templer (US Embassy), Anukool Bhatnagar, and Sandeep Kamat (Bosch Nepal).

4. Informal Interaction Programs

Convened India-Nepal fintech stakeholders for dialogue on seamless cross-border QR transactions, helping sustain collaboration before market adoption.

5. Startup Summit Nepal 2024

Held 17 Dec 2024 at Aloft Kathmandu Thamel with cross-border policy and startup focus; Taximandu won Best Startup of the Year 2024 (NPR 30,000).

6. Startup Summit Nepal 2023

Held 9 Sep 2023 with global-perspective startup theme, panels, pitching, and networking; Intern Nepal won the pitching contest.

7. Partnership with DMAE, Pulchowk Campus

Academic ecosystem collaboration to bridge student innovation, research, and startup execution pathways.

8. Startup Summit Nepal 2025

National-level conference themed "Fostering Global Collaboration, Building Beyond Borders" with focus on finance, mentorship, regulation, and global market integration.

9. AI Hackathon 2026

24-hour national AI hackathon at Embark College, Pulchowk (15-16 Mar 2026). NagarikAPI emerged as overall winner with an impactful public-service-focused AI solution.
